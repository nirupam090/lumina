# User Personas

Based on explicit persona differentiation requested in the story planning phase, we identify two core student archetypes that interact uniquely with Lumina's behavioral nudges and productivity tools.

## Persona 1: The Regimented Planner (Alex)
- **Background**: 3rd-year Engineering Student operating under an extremely tight schedule.
- **Characteristics**: Highly organized, goal-oriented, proactive, prefers deep-work states.
- **Pain Points**: Needs seamless syncing across devices, granular offline control for uninterrupted deep-focus study (RAG), and advanced structural breakdown in the Kanban engine.
- **Lumina Usage**: Relies heavily on the exactness of the Timetable Parser, the zero-latency of Local-First RAG, and views the Cognitive Debt Score as a competitive leaderboard metric among squads.

## Persona 2: The Distraction-Prone Student (Sam)
- **Background**: 2nd-year Engineering Student struggling with high workload and task paralysis.
- **Characteristics**: Tends to procrastinate, loses track of deadlines, needs social accountability to remain focused.
- **Pain Points**: Easily switches tabs yielding high context switching, often forgets to manually log attendance, misses crucial assignment deadlines.
- **Lumina Usage**: Benefits primarily from the ContextSwitch Engine nudges, the automated Bunk Analytics to know exactly when a skip is "safe", and Study Squads for external group motivation.
